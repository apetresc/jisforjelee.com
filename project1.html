Harbin Ice and Snow Festival

This communication design project for the Harbin International Ice & Snow Festival includes the design of a new identity, 18"x22" poster, event pamphlet, and admission tickets. All the items in this bilingual package include information in both Chinese and English. Promoting the festival's themes of appreciation for nature and transformation, the pamphlet and tickets can be turned into trinkets and other useful everyday items.
