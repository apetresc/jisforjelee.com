Watt's in your Pocket?

This project delivers the design of a multifunctional product package for light bulbs. Playing on the witty name, "Watt's in Your Pocket?", this multilingual package design presents the product in reusable denim pockets that can be used as fanny packs or wall organizers when strung together by connecting one pocket to the other via buttons on the back. The label itself can also be refolded into connectable organizers.
