BYOL: Bring Your Own Lunch

An advocacy piece encouraging people to pack lunches rather than eat out. With each unfold of the pamphlet, viewers can learn about the advantages of packed lunches. This information design project folds out into a poster with a nutrition and grocery guide along with several recipe ideas, showing people how easy and great it is to bring their own lunches to school or work.
