Delia Group of Schools

Going into 50 years of education excellence next year, Delia Group of Schools in Hong Kong is looking for a new, fresh look. Keeping their existing logo, the update of the school board's brand identity includes the redesign of stationery and school websites. To differentiate the groups within the school board (e.g. Primary and Kindegarten, High School), each group is given its own colour while keeping with the imagery and style of the overall brand. These designs will all be rolled out into production for the coming school year.
