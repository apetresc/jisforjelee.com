Hello, I'm Jelee.

Well, actually - I'm Jennifer Lee, and I am a graphic designer.

As an environmentalist, I constantly look for opportunities to reuse objects in multiple ways. As a traveler, I am inspired by multiculturalism. As an enthusiastic learner, I enjoy exploring the new possibilities the world of interactivity offers. Everything in life influences us to become a certain person with certain values. The endless sources of inspiration around me have influenced me to design, and I am determined to produce things that will have a positive impact on society through imaginative thinking and creative problem solving.

Outside of design, I also enjoy photography, fashion design, baking, singing in the shower, and going on long, long bike rides. I love bread, funky socks, and squirrels. Nice to meet you!
